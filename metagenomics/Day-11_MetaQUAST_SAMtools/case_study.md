# Case Study

# Quality Assessment of a Metagenome Assembly

## Background

A metagenomic dataset was assembled using MEGAHIT.

Before genome binning, the assembly quality and read alignments needed to be evaluated.

---

## Problem

Poor-quality assemblies and incorrectly processed alignment files can reduce the accuracy of downstream analyses.

---

## Solution

MetaQUAST was used to assess assembly quality, and SAMtools converted, sorted, and indexed alignment files.

---

## Outcome

The processed BAM files and MetaQUAST reports provided a reliable foundation for genome binning and quality assessment.

---

## Conclusion

Assembly evaluation and alignment processing are essential preprocessing steps in genome-resolved metagenomics.
